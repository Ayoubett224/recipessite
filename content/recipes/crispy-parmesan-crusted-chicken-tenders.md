## Ingredients  
- 2 chicken tenders (400g)  
- 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 tsp garlic powder  
- 1 tsp paprika  
- Salt and pepper to taste  

## Instructions  
1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).  
2. Mix cheese, oil, spices, and tenders.  
3. Arrange on baking sheet.  
4. Bake 20 minutes until golden.  
5. Rest 5 minutes before serving.  
6. Pair with sides or soups.  

## Pro Tips  
- Use frozen tenders for convenience.  
- Substitute breadcrumbs if Parmesan is unavailable.  
- Add a drizzle of honey for extra sweetness.  

## Storage & Leftovers  
Store in airtight container up to 2 days. Reheat in oven for best crispness.
