## Ingredients  
- 1 cup oats  
- 1/2 cup almond milk  
- 1/4 cup honey  
- 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips  
- 1 tsp cinnamon  
- 1/4 cup peanut butter  

## Instructions  
1. Mix oats, almond milk, honey, chocolate chips, cinnamon, and peanut butter thoroughly.  
2. Press mixture into a pan, smooth the surface.  
3. Chill for 30 minutes before slicing into squares.  

## Pro Tips  
- Use full-fat dairy for richer flavor.  
- Swap nuts for seeds if preferred.  
- Store airtight to maintain freshness.  

## Storage & Leftovers  
Enjoy fresh or refrigerate for up to 5 days. Reheat gently if needed.
