## Ingredients

*   1 1/2 cups Rolled Oats (Old Fashioned)
*   1 1/2 cups Milk (Almond, Oat, or Dairy)
*   1/2 cup Greek Yogurt (Plain)
*   1/4 cup Maple Syrup
*   2 tablespoons Chia Seeds
*   1 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
*   1/2 teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
*   1/4 teaspoon Ground Cloves
*   1 medium Apple, peeled, cored, and diced
*   1/4 cup Chopped Walnuts (optional)



## Instructions

1.  Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 8x8 inch baking dish.
2.  In a large bowl, combine the rolled oats, milk, Greek yogurt, maple syrup, chia seeds, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Stir well to ensure everything is combined.
3.  Add the diced apple to the oat mixture and stir gently to distribute evenly.
4.  Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.
5.  Sprinkle the chopped walnuts (if using) over the top.
6.  Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the oats are set and the top is golden brown.  A toothpick inserted into the center should come out clean.
7.  Let cool slightly before serving.



## Pro Tips

*   **Overnight Oats Variation:** For an even quicker breakfast, combine all ingredients in a jar the night before and refrigerate.  Enjoy cold or warm it up in the microwave.
*   **Spice it Up:** Add a pinch of cardamom or ginger for a different flavor profile.
*   **Fruit Swap:** Feel free to substitute the apple with other fruits like berries, peaches, or bananas.



## Storage & Leftovers

Store leftover baked oats in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the microwave for 30-60 seconds.
