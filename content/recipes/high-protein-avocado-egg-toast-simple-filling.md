This High-Protein Avocado Egg Toast: Simple & Filling recipe is a game-changer for busy mornings. Combining creamy avocado, fluffy eggs, and toasted bread, it’s a delicious way to start your day. Packed with protein to keep you full longer, it’s perfect for busy professionals, athletes, or anyone craving a nutritious meal without the fuss. Whether you’re prepping for the week or just need a quick bite, this recipe delivers flavor and fuel in under 15 minutes. The best part? It’s customizable—add spinach, feta, or chili flakes to suit your taste.  

## Why You'll Love This Recipe  
- **High-protein fuel**: Combines eggs and avocado for a satisfying 25g+ of protein per serving.  
- **Zero complicated steps**: No fancy gadgets needed—just a pan and a toaster.  
- **Filling and balanced**: Healthy fats from avocado keep you satisfied until lunch.  
- **Customizable**: Add veggies, protein, or spices to make it uniquely yours.  

## Ingredients  
- 2 ripe avocados  
- 4 large eggs  
- 8 slices whole-grain or sourdough bread  
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- 1/2 tsp salt  
- 1/4 tsp black pepper  
- 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes (halved, optional)  
- 1/4 cup spinach (fresh or frozen, optional)  
- 1/2 cup cooked bacon or sausage (optional)  
- 1 tbsp lemon juice  
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh herbs (cilantro or parsley, optional)  

## Instructions  
1. **Toast the bread**: Lightly toast 4 slices of bread in a toaster or skillet until golden. Set aside.  
2. **Prepare avocado**: Mash 1 avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.  
3. **Cook eggs**: In a pan over medium heat, cook 2 eggs with olive oil until fully set. Repeat for remaining eggs.  
4. **Assemble**: Spread avocado on two slices of toast. Top with cooked eggs. Add optional ingredients like tomatoes, spinach, or bacon.  
5. **Season**: Drizzle lemon juice and herbs over each toast.  
6. **Serve**: Enjoy immediately while warm.  

## Pro Tips  
- **Boost fiber**: Use whole-grain bread or add chia seeds to the avocado spread.  
- **Make ahead**: Prep avocado and eggs separately, then assemble before serving.  
- **Spice it up**: Add chili flakes, hot sauce, or sriracha for a flavor kick.  

## Storage & Leftovers  
Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 days. Reheat gently in the toaster or microwave.
