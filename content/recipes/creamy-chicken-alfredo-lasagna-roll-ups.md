This Creamy Chicken Alfredo Lasagna Roll-Ups recipe is your go-to for a speedy yet satisfying meal. It's an easy dinner recipe that's ready in just over half an hour, making it ideal for busy days. The combination of tender chicken and creamy Alfredo sauce creates a memorable taste that elevates any gathering.

What makes this recipe special is its balance of rich ingredients and simple technique. You'll love the creamy sauce, crispy roll-ups, and the perfect harmony of flavors. This dish is perfect for a cozy evening or a quick weeknight dinner.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb boneless chicken thighs, cubed
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup Alfredo sauce
- 1 cup ricotta cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
- 2 cups lasagna noodles
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions
1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the chicken thighs until golden brown.
2. Remove the chicken and set aside, reserving the sauce.
3. In the same skillet, add a splash of olive oil and sauté minced garlic with honey until fragrant.
4. Stir in red pepper flakes, oregano, salt, and pepper.
5. Pour the cream into the skillet and simmer until slightly thickened.
6. Mix ricotta with basil, then combine with Alfredo sauce and chicken mixture.
7. Layer lasagna noodles in a baking dish, spreading the mixture evenly.
8. Top with lasagna noodles, cheese, and the chicken sauce.
9. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
10. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

## Pro Tips
- For a richer flavor, add a bit more honey to the sauce.
- Substitute almond flour for regular flour if you prefer a gluten-free version.
- This recipe works beautifully with a side of garlic bread or crusty bread.

## Storage & Leftovers
This recipe stores well in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Simply reheat gently and enjoy! It's perfect for meal prep.
