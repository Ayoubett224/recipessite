This Spinach Feta Wrap recipe is designed to be both satisfying and simple. It combines tender spinach with the tangy depth of feta, creating a balance that's surprisingly robust without being heavy. The result is a dish that's perfect for those who appreciate bold flavors yet want a healthy option. This recipe is ideal for busy weeknights when you still want to impress your family with a flavorful meal.

What makes this recipe special is its unique blend of ingredients that work together harmoniously. The fresh spinach adds a vibrant green color and subtle earthiness, while the feta brings a salty, creamy richness. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned cook, this Spinach Feta Wrap is easy to adapt and customize to your preferences.

## Ingredients
- 4 whole wheat tortillas
- 3 cups fresh spinach
- 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp honey
- Fresh herbs (parsley, basil)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
2. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted, then stir in feta cheese and lemon juice.
3. Warm the whole wheat tortilla and spread a thin layer of the spinach feta mixture inside.
4. Roll up the tortilla tightly and slice into wedges or serve directly.
5. Serve with a drizzle of honey for extra sweetness and a sprinkle of parsley for garnish.
6. Pair with a side salad for a balanced meal that's both light and satisfying.

## Pro Tips
- For extra protein, add grilled chicken to the wrap.
- If you prefer a lighter texture, use a low-carb tortilla alternative.
- This recipe is perfect for meal prepping, making it a quick and easy dinner option.

## Storage & Leftovers
Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently to restore the perfect warmth.
